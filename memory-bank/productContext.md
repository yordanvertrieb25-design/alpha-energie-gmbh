# Product Context

## Purpose
The website acts as the primary marketing and informational tool for Alpha Energie GmbH. It positions the company as a dynamic, personal, and modern player in the energy market.

## Problems Solved
- Educates users on energy optimization.
- Facilitates the recruitment of sales partners.
- Highlights the company's culture and benefits (e.g., "Great Place To Work" certification, "Alpha Energie cares" program).

## How it works
- Single-page or multi-section landing page design featuring smooth scrolling.
- Contains specific sections for Solutions, About, Partnerships, Careers, and News.
