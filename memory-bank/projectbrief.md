# Project Brief: Alpha Energie GmbH Website

## Core Requirements
- Create and maintain a modern, engaging website for Alpha Energie GmbH, an energy distribution company operating since 2015.
- The website must appeal to three main target groups:
  1. Consumers looking to save energy costs.
  2. Sales partners (Handelsvertreter) looking for a reliable network and strong energy products.
  3. Energy suppliers needing a sales network.

## Goals
- Serve as the digital hub for Alpha Energie GmbH.
- Attract and recruit new sales partners.
- Provide a premium, dynamic user experience with modern aesthetics (glassmorphism, vibrant orange brand color, smooth animations).
